KEAN UNIVERSITY
LEAVE OF ABSENCE POLICY

GENERAL POLICY
Students who must temporarily leave the University for medical or personal reasons may request a leave of absence.

MEDICAL LEAVE
Medical leaves are processed through the Kean Wellness Center, including:
- Student Health Services
- Counseling Center

Students requesting a Health and Wellness Absence should review official withdrawal procedures.

MILITARY LEAVE
Military personnel called to active duty must:
- Provide activation orders to the Center for Veteran Student Services (CVSS).

Students returning from active duty must contact the Office of Veteran Student Services for readmission.

RETURNING FROM LEAVE
Students returning from a pre-approved leave must contact the Center for Veteran Student Services (CVSS) to complete the return process.
