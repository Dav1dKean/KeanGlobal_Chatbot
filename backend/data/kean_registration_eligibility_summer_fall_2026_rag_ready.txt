OVERVIEW
==================================================

This document explains who is eligible to register for Summer 2026 and Fall 2026 at Kean University and when each student group can begin registration.

General eligibility rules:
- Degree-seeking undergraduate students are eligible for registration if they have taken classes within the last three semesters.
- Undergraduate students who have not been enrolled for three consecutive semesters are not eligible to register and must apply for readmission.
- Degree-seeking graduate and doctoral students are eligible for registration if they have taken classes within the last three semesters.
- Graduate and doctoral students who have not been enrolled for three consecutive semesters, but fewer than six semesters, are not eligible to register and must apply for readmission or reactivation.
- Graduate and doctoral students who have not been enrolled for six or more semesters must reapply for admission.
- All students should seek academic advisement and resolve any outstanding holds before registering.

IMPORTANT RULES AND NOTES
==================================================

Priority registration designation:
- Priority registration status is designated as of April 2, 2026.
- Students whose status changes after April 2 must have their designation updated by providing valid documentation to the Office of the Registrar at regme@kean.edu.

Students with a disability:
- Students with a disability who seek support services must be on file in the Office of Accessibility Services, Downs Hall, Room 122, in order to register with priority access.

Late registration fee:
- A 105 dollar late registration fee applies to students registering after the published payment due date.
- Newly admitted students accepted within two weeks of that date or later are exempt.
- The fee does not apply to course changes made after the due date.

Overload authorization:
- Overload authorization will not be accepted for closed courses.

Course format note:
- These dates and times represent full-term and first-half in-person courses.
- Students should view the Academic Calendar for important dates and deadlines for all course formats.

Official update note:
- Registration schedule is subject to change.
- Students are encouraged to regularly visit Kean registration pages for updates.

SEARCH-FRIENDLY FAQ STYLE FACTS
==================================================

Question: When does Fall 2026 registration start for undergraduates at Kean?
Answer: Priority undergraduate registration begins Tuesday, April 14, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET. General continuing undergraduate registration for all students with 0 credits and above begins Wednesday, April 22, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET.

Question: When does Summer 2026 registration start for undergraduates at Kean?
Answer: Priority undergraduate registration begins Tuesday, April 14, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET. General continuing undergraduate registration for all students with 0 credits and above begins Wednesday, April 22, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET and runs through Wednesday, May 6, 2026.

Question: When does Fall 2026 registration start for graduate students at Kean?
Answer: Priority graduate and doctoral registration begins Tuesday, April 14, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET. All continuing graduate and doctoral students with 0 credits and above may register beginning Thursday, April 16, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET.

Question: When does Summer 2026 registration start for graduate students at Kean?
Answer: Priority graduate and doctoral registration begins Tuesday, April 14, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET. All continuing graduate and doctoral students with 0 credits and above may register beginning Thursday, April 16, 2026 at 6:00 AM ET.

Question: Who gets priority registration at Kean?
Answer: Priority registration groups include students with a disability, veterans, and Kean athletes. Undergraduate priority registration also includes scholars with a 3.85 GPA and above.

Question: What happens if an undergraduate student has not enrolled for three consecutive semesters?
Answer: The undergraduate student is not eligible to register and must apply for readmission.

Question: What happens if a graduate or doctoral student has not enrolled for three consecutive semesters?
Answer: The graduate or doctoral student is not eligible to register and must apply for readmission or reactivation. If the student has not been enrolled for six or more semesters, the student must reapply for admission.

Question: Do students need to clear holds before registering?
Answer: Yes. All students should seek academic advisement and resolve outstanding holds before registering.
