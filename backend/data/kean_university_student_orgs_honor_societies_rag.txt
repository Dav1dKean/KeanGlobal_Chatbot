HONOR SOCIETY ELIGIBILITY / PROCESS EXAMPLES
Kean maintains a PDF overview of honor society eligibility and induction information. Examples visible on the official 2026 honors/cords contact document include:
- Phi Alpha Theta (History): students must hold a 3.3 overall GPA, have taken 45 credits with at least 12 in History at Kean, and have History as a first or second major; invitations are sent in February with induction in April.
- Mu Kappa Tau (Marketing): students must complete 50 percent of coursework toward the marketing major, have at least a 3.25 GPA, and be in the top 10 percent of their class.
- Lambda Alpha Sigma: recognition for juniors or seniors with at least 56 completed credits, including at least 32 at Kean, and qualifying GPA standards based on the calendar year's coursework.
- Phi Kappa Phi: the PDF lists competitive academic ranking criteria for juniors and seniors and notes conduct/integrity requirements.
- Upsilon Pi Epsilon: the Kean entry says undergraduate candidates in computing and information disciplines must complete at least 60 semester hours and have at least a 3.45 GPA; graduate candidates must complete at least 9 semester hours of graduate credit and have at least a 3.45 GPA.
