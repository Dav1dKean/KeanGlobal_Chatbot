[SECTION] Overview
Kean University will celebrate the Class of 2026 on Wednesday, May 20, 2026.
The commencement ceremony is for both undergraduate and graduate students.
The event begins at 8:30 a.m.
The location is Prudential Center in Newark, New Jersey.

[SECTION] Cap and Gown
To participate in the commencement procession, students must wear official Kean University academic regalia.
Required regalia includes cap, gown, tassel, stole, and hood.
Students can purchase regalia inside Kean's Barnes & Noble Bookstore using Flex Dollars.
Students can also purchase regalia online using other forms of credit.

[SECTION] Eligible Graduates and Regalia Costs
Kean University covers most of the regalia cost for bachelor's and master's students.
Bachelor's degree candidates are responsible for 30 dollars.
Master's degree candidates are responsible for 40 dollars.
Regalia ordering link is provided through Oak Hall.

[SECTION] Bachelor and Master Regalia Deadlines
Ship to Kean for Grad Fair Pick-Up deadline: Friday, March 27, 2026.
Ship to Home deadline: Monday, April 13, 2026.

[SECTION] Doctoral Regalia
Two types of doctoral regalia are available.
Option 1: Souvenir Regalia. This is designed for one-time use and is suitable for graduates who do not expect to wear doctoral regalia again.
Option 2: Fine Quality Custom Kean Regalia. This is a premium option recommended for individuals pursuing academic careers who may wear regalia in future commencement ceremonies.
Detailed ordering information is provided through Oak Hall.

[SECTION] Graduate Fair Overview
At the Graduate Fair, graduates can pick up regalia and stoles and ask commencement-related questions.
The following departments will be present to assist students.
Registrar's Office: verify graduation status.
Career Services: access job placement assistance and career resources.
Alumni Engagement: register for future alumni events.

[SECTION] Graduate Fair at Kean Ocean
Event name: Kean Ocean Graduate Fair.
Date: Wednesday, April 29, 2026.
Time: 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Location: Gateway Building Atrium, Kean Ocean Campus.

[SECTION] Graduate Fair at Kean Union
Event name: Kean Union Graduate Fair.
Date: Thursday, April 30, 2026.
Time: 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Location: Barnes and Noble Bookstore, Green Lane Academic Building, Kean Union campus.

[SECTION] Senior Portraits Overview
Graduates from Fall 2025, Spring 2026, and Summer 2026 in undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs can schedule a portrait sitting appointment.
Portraits may be used for professional networking, job searches, and profile photos.
There is no sitting fee.
After reviewing proofs, graduates may choose whether to purchase photos.
Graduates should arrive in business attire.
Cap and gown attire will be provided for a second pose.

[SECTION] Senior Portraits at Kean Ocean
Location: Gateway Building, 5th Floor, Room 500, Kean Ocean campus.
Appointment dates include Tuesday, March 24, 2026, from 9:30 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Appointment dates include Wednesday, March 25, 2026, from 12 p.m. to 8 p.m.
These are listed as the last appointments for the Kean Ocean campus.
Appointments are scheduled through the Kean Ocean portrait booking link.

[SECTION] Senior Portraits at Kean Union
Location: Miron Student Center, Room 228, Kean Union campus.
Appointment dates include Monday, March 30, 2026, from 9:30 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Appointment dates include Tuesday, March 31, 2026, from 9:30 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Appointment dates include Wednesday, April 1, 2026, from 9:30 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Appointment dates include Thursday, April 2, 2026, from 9:30 a.m. to 5 p.m.
The last appointment date for the Kean Union campus will be at the Graduate Fair on Thursday, April 30, 2026, with limited appointments.
The page states that these later appointments will not be available until after the March and April portrait sittings, and an email will be sent when they become available.
Appointments are scheduled through the Kean Union portrait booking link.
